We welcome back Bradley Marshall who graduated with a First Class Honours Degree in BSc (Hons) Software Engineering from Solent University in 2022.

Bradley works as a Control Systems Engineer at Ocean Infinity. The vastness of the ocean interests him more and more when working for a company that is transforming operations at sea, to enable people and the planet to thrive.

He wants to create truthful and meaningful products, that will generally assist people in their lives. Bradley has an immense interest in the design of software; as well as the usability and functionality of products, both via: software, and hardware. "Just find the fact that a collection of 1's and 0's can do so much - absolutely fascinating - which is why I wanted to become a Software Engineer; so that I can be the person to make the magic of computers happen."
