Happy Hour 2022

Happy Hour Digital Industry Talks are designed to offer an insight into working in the tech and digital industries. Talks are mostly focused on students,  "early career" or how to get into the industry with speakers presenting their journey into the industry and professional practice.

Some weeks we will have one or two speakers and panel discussions - each event fits into a 1 to 1.15 hour frame (5pm-6.15pm) on Thursdays in Spark Building, Solent University, East Park Terrace, Southampton, SO14 0YN. We normally continue the discussion in O’Neill's pub 130-132 Above Bar Street so please do join us after each event if you are able to.
