About

Solent University Computing Subject Group's Happy Hour Talks offer insights to working within the digital and tech industry by bringing in practitioners, including Solent Alumni, to share their professional journeys.


Happy Hour talks take place on Thursdays 5pm - 6.15pm (university term time only) in Spark Building, Solent University, East Park Terrace, Southampton, S014 0YN.

Happy Hour Talk Sessions last 1 to 1.5 hours although we will aim to wrap up by 5.15pm. Some weeks there will be more than one speaker/activity and we normally continue the discussion in the pub so please do join us after this event if you are able to.

For more details or if work in the digital/tech industry and would like to do a talk, please contact martin.reid@solent.ac.uk
and follow us on Twitter @WebDevSolent
